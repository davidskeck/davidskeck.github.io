"Anything You Want" - Book review

Jul 7, 2014

Anything You Want - Derek Sivers

I had the chance to read this book about a week ago. I expected to just read the first few pages before heading into work, but I found it so enthralling that I read the whole thing that morning. It only took about an hour, just like Derek said it would in the first chapter.

As you can tell, this book is pretty short. The content feels almost obsessively pared down to the most relevant message possible.

I love this book is because its voice is so lucid. Such clarity is something I admire and want to learn myself.

At the end of the book, Sivers invites anyone who has “made it this far” to email him with any comments or questions. I took advantage of this offer and I received an email back the very next day. It is obvious he cares about his readers. It was comforting to see that Sivers embodies the qualities he discusses in this book.

10/10
